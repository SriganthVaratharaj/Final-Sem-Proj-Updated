=== FONT VARIANT CONFUSION MAP ===
# In thick/decorative Bengali fonts (common in commercial invoices):
# Format: ACTUAL_CHARACTER → commonly misread as

VOWELS:
- অ → misread as: ও, এ
- আ → misread as: অ+া separate
- ই / ঈ → often confused with each other
- উ / ঊ → often confused with each other
- ো (o-matra) → misread as: ো or ো combinations

CONSONANTS:
- ব  (ba) → misread as: ভ, ব in decorative fonts look similar
- ভ  (bha)→ misread as: ব, ঘ
- ণ  (na) → misread as: ন, গ
- ঠ  (tha)→ misread as: ট, ড
- ঢ  (dha)→ misread as: ড, ঘ
- শ  (sha)→ misread as: ষ, স
- ষ  (sha)→ misread as: শ, স
- ঘ  (gha)→ misread as: গ, ধ
- ঝ  (jha)→ misread as: জ, হ
- ফ  (pha)→ misread as: ব, প
- ল  (la) → misread as: ল (doubled)
- হ  (ha) → misread as: ঘ, য in thick fonts

HASANTA and CONJUNCTS:
- ্ (hasanta/virama) — critical for conjuncts, often missed
- ক্ষ (ksha), জ্ঞ (gya) — complex conjuncts often misread
