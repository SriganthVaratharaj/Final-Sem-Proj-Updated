=== FONT VARIANT CONFUSION MAP ===
# In thick/bold/decorative Devanagari fonts (common in Indian bills/posters):
# Format: DECORATIVE_APPEARANCE → ACTUAL_CHARACTER
# Use this to correct misidentified characters.

VOWELS:
- Rounded thick "अ" can look like: ओ, ए
- Decorative "इ" can look like: ई or ि
- Stylized "ऊ" can look like: उ or ऊ

CONSONANTS (most commonly misread in bold fonts):
- व  (va)  → often misread as: ब, भ, फ
- थ  (tha) → often misread as: ध, द
- ण  (na)  → often misread as: ग, ज
- ठ  (tha) → often misread as: ट, ड
- ढ  (dha) → often misread as: ड, ग
- श  (sha) → often misread as: ष, स
- ष  (sha) → often misread as: श, स
- ञ  (nya) → often misread as: ज, न
- घ  (gha) → often misread as: ग, ध
- झ  (jha) → often misread as: ज, ह
- फ  (pha) → often misread as: व, ब, प
- ख  (kha) → often misread as: क, ग
- ल  (la)  → often misread as: ल (doubled) or ल+ा
- ह  (ha)  → often misread as: य, र in thick fonts

CONJUNCTS (most common in bills):
- विद्युत → electricity (common in power bills)
- बिजली  → electricity
- विथूल  → can appear as: फाजीकर्म (decorative rounded font)
- ग्राहक → customer (common in utility bills)
- कालावधि → period
- युनिट   → unit
- शुल्क   → charge/fee
- तारीख  → date
- कुल    → total
- देय    → payable

=== COMMON BILL KEYWORDS (Hindi) ===
Invoice/Bill: बिल, चालान, रसीद, पावती
Customer: ग्राहक, उपभोक्ता
Date: तारीख, दिनांक
Amount: राशि, रकम
Total: कुल, कुल राशि, योग
Tax: कर, जीएसटी
Units: युनिट, इकाई
Due: देय, बकाया
Period: अवधि, कालावधि
